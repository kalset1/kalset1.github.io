About Me

I'm a fourth year student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, pursuing an additional major in Robotics. I'm excited to learn about computer systems and hardware design, and enjoy applying this knowledge to the realm of robotics. Throughout my time at CMU, I've worked with the Biorobotics Lab, TA'ed Introduction to Computer Systems (18-213), programmed Franka Panda robot arms, developed an RTOS for an STM32 microcontroller, interned at Apple as an FPGA Engineer for Hardware Test Engineering, wrote a compiler, and designed a processor. In my spare time, I enjoy making music, rock climbing, and reading.
