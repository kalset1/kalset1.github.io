About Me

I'm a third year student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, pursuing an additional major in Robotics. I'm excited to learn about computer systems and hardware design, and enjoy applying this knowledge to the realm of robotics. Throughout my time at CMU, I've worked with the Biorobotics Lab, TA'ed Introduction to Computer Systems (18-213), synthesized and wrote code for FPGAs, programmed Franka Panda robot arms, and developed an RTOS for an STM32 microcontroller. In my spare time, I enjoy making music, rock climbing, and reading.
